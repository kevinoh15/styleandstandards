Color

Color plays a very important part in the overall look and feel of our site. It also helps reinforce certain behaviors for the user if done consistently. For instance, the color red may always alert them something urgent has occurred even if no words are on screen. Or a shade of gray blending in with a white background could tell the user that an item is not active. Detailed here are the colors most commonly associated with ChathamDirect along with their purpose and what we hope they will teach our users.

  • Black

    Primary use is as the site's font color and navigation background.

    rgba(65, 65, 65, 1.0)

    #414141

  • Dark Gray

    Used for borders and certain icon colors.

    rgba(169, 170, 174, 1.0)

    #a9aaae

  • Medium Gray

    Primary use is as a hover color for certain buttons.

    rgba(210, 210, 210, 1.0)

    #d2d2d2

  • Gray

    Used on sidebar tabs and some buttons.

    rgba(222, 223, 227, 1.0)

    #dedfe3

  • Light Gray

    Mainly used drop down menus and alternating rows.

    rgba(238, 239, 242, 1.0)

    #eeeff2

  • Blue

    Highlights items that are currently selected.

    rgba(171, 200, 230, 1.0)

    #abc8e6

  • Green

    Typically used for progression buttons and positive actions.

    rgba(115, 173, 85, 1.0)

    #73ad55

  • Yellow

    Used as a soft alert color.

    rgba(235, 172, 85, 1.0)

    #ebac20

  • Red

    Used on negative actions and strong alerts.

    rgba(156, 33, 64, 1.0)

    #9c2140
